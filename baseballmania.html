Sports Game

Soccer

Instructions: The goal of the game is to try to score as many runs as possible before getting 3 outs. You get outs by hitting the red zones or getting a strike

Controls: Press Space to Swing, Press up arrow to Pitch, Press 1, 2, or 3 to change righty batter skins, Press 4, 5, or 6 to change lefty batter skins, Press 7, 8, or 9 to change pitcher skins
